Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at New York University, proudly advised by Prof. Qiaoyu Tan. My current research focuses on Agentic Reasoning, Graph Learning, and Multimodal LLMs.

Previously, I received my M.S. in Computer Engineering from NYU in 2022 and B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Northeastern University (China) in 2020.
